Welcome to AutoChannel

What is AutoChannel?

AutoChannel is desinged to make managing voice channels much easier. This allows Admins/High ranking moderators to manage which categories are enabled to have voice channels popualated.

This is done by enabling autochannel for a specified category and setting unique "prefix". The prefix by default will use "AC!". It is recommended that the prefix never overlap none of the static channels you created. If the prefix of your manually created channels are the same as your static/hand crafted channels, then the bot will act as they are part of its automation.

Currently AutoChannel bot will always keep 1 empty channel free. This means it will scale up and down if you join an auto generated channel in an enabled category
