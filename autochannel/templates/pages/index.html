Welcome to AutoChannel

What is AutoChannel?

AutoChannel is here for Admins/moderators/channel managers to facilitate managing voice channels. This can help you moderate small to large discord servers with over 1 million+ active users.

Auto-chan (AutoChannels friendly bot!) does this by watching categories you specified to always have empty channels. You can tell Auto-chan to watch specific categories and always make sure it has 1-10 empty channels and you can set how many users can be in each channel generated. You might think but how does this make my life easier? This means you will no longer have excess channels and not have to scroll endless looking for groups or see how many rooms are occupied.

Auto-chan also checks to make sure you don't have too many channels in your categories. If people leave it will automatically clean up empty channels.
